The Author

Carlos Perez who is also the author of "The Deeplearning PlayBook". has been a professional software architect and developer focused on developing software systems from concept to production, since 1993. Mr. Perez has 20 years experience developing software. Since 2000, he has primarily held technical architect roles in software development. He was the Principal Architect and Director for a converged mobile phone product for parental control. He was the head architect in a venture capital funded start-up company developing optimization solutions for B2B exchanges. Mr. Perez has worked for IBM Corporation's Internet Division and T.J. Watson Research. He has a diverse experience working in several industries and US federal agencies. These include the National Cancer Institute, Fannie Mae, Department of Education, Social Security Administration, Nextel and Frequentis.
